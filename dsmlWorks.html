Predicting Depression in Students Using Machine Learning

By Ronnel Bermas, Justine Evora, and Christine Sotoza

This app processes student mental health survey data and builds machine learning models to predict whether a student is likely to have depression. It employs K-Nearest Neighbors (KNN) and Decision Tree algorithms for classification.
